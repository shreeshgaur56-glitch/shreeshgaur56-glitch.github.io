This platform provides concise information on tree species of the ICFRE–FRI campus in Dehradun, including taxonomy, uses, and locations on an interactive map.

How this website works

Data are managed in a structured Airtable base and displayed here as species cards and map markers. As new species and locations are added to the database, the website updates automatically.
